Brownie Recipe

Craving a decadent, fudgy treat that's both easy to make and utterly irresistible? Look no further! This brownie recipe delivers the perfect balance of rich chocolate flavor, a moist, chewy center, and a delightful crackly top. Get ready to indulge in brownie perfection!

Ingredients & Measurements

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of cocoa powder
  • 1/2 tsp of slat
  • 1/2 tsp of baking powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp of vanilla
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (melted)
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 3/4 cup of flour
  • 2 tsp of espresso powder
  • 1 cup of chocolate chips

Step by Step Instructions

  1. Mix together eggs, cocoa powder, salt, baking powder, instant espresso, and vanilla
  2. In a seperate bowl cream butter snd sugar, then microwave gradually (15 sec at a time) until sugar dissolves and is shiny
  3. Combine the egg and butter mixtures well
  4. Add flour and mix gently, do not overmix
  5. Add chocolate chips and mix until combined
  6. Pour into 9x9 pan and bake at 350 degrees for 28-30 minutes
